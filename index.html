What we offer

We offer a 2-day in person bootcamp with a focus on

  • Delivery pipelines
  • Deploying to the cloud
  • Infrastructure automation
  • Perfomance and scaling
  • Effective developer workflows
  • Effective testing

Participants will create a continuous delivery pipeline for a real-world application and deploy it to Amazon Web Services.

We emphasize a culture of continous delivery and pragmatic automation.

Our bootcamps are highly interactive, hands on and based on the socratic method of learning.

Mohit Thatte
